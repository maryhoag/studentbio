how to create an open-faced avacado and egg white sandwich

  1. whisk together egg whites and a pinch of salt and pepper
  2. scramble egg whites until set
  3. toast a piece of whole grain bread
  4. top toast with thinly sliced avacado
  5. add egg whites
  6. top with chopped bacon and feta cheese
